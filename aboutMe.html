Hey My People, I'm Keshon! I was born and raised in Lancaster, California, and I always had a fascination for technology. Even as a kid, I was always surrounded by technology like DVD/VCR players, BlackBerry phones, and Motorola Razrs from 2005. I found myself drawn to how these devices worked, especially when the first iPhone came out. But it was when we got our first home computer that my curiosity really took off. I spent hours in the loft of my house exploring how to create designs, playing around with Photoshop (back when it was free!), and building fun layouts for my MySpace page. I loved the creative freedom that technology gave me.

But you know, even with all of this, I sometimes wondered if technology was truly my calling. After serving in the military, I found myself pulled back into the world of computers, diving into e-commerce, affiliate marketing, and digital design. I enjoyed learning new things, but I still felt like there was a piece missing. I wanted something that felt more like me, something that really sparked my passion.

Then, one day, I got the chance to build a website for someone, and everything changed. I was so excited to finally put my skills to use, but I didn’t want to just use a standard website builder like Wix or WordPress. I wanted to really build something from scratch. After days of searching for the right approach, I came across Rich Armstrong's course on Skill Share on how to hand-code a website using HTML and CSS. That course opened my eyes to what broader possibilities that are in design, and suddenly everything clicked. I knew this was the path for me.

If you're curious about web design or anything technology-related, I'd love to connect with you.
