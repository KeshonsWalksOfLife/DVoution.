Christian Worldview

My core beliefs are spiritual in nature. Do I believe in God? Absolutely. Do I believe Jesus died on the cross for me to live, breathe, and see another day? Without a doubt. I believe that God the Father is inside each and every one of us. We are created in His likeness and flow in His nature as beings like Him. To me, this means that we are the true controllers of our lives, empowered by God’s spirit. My life is a reflection of the thoughts I have allowed myself to believe are true.

From my perspective, God is all about life and loving thy neighbor, regardless of feelings or emotions that may arise. God is the creator of many things, but nature and humanity are His greatest creations. As humans, we can create from the same divine inspiration, using the tools and resources already given to us. These tools are within our reach, but it is up to each person to realize this within themselves. The "I"—our true essence—is the blessing of life. The "I" must find purpose, which in turn brings meaning to God and Jesus Christ, leading to fulfillment and everlasting life.

I've found communities through Reddit and connections with others who feel drawn to this path, but many resources I've encountered haven't fully guided me in this direction. Much of my journey has been through self-discovery. This has given me purpose, motivating me to become my best self. While understanding right and wrong, I've come to see that these concepts are polar opposites shaped by upbringing and environment. Right and wrong are often subjective, influenced by individual beliefs and perspectives.

Through these experiences, my faith in God has become unwavering. Trusting Him is something I do without question. I've seen His work in my life, helping me remember who I am and showing me His goodness time and time again. I have transformed from a place of no hope to a place of limitless possibilities. My life is truly filled with joy because that is what I believe, and it hasn’t let me down.
